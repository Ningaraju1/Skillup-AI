Background Context

SkillUp AI is a resume builder and compliance checker that helps experienced professionals and senior executives create resumes that get more interviews. It strikes the right balance between professionalism and originality, combining a clean structure with thoughtful storytelling elements that work for people and pass through applicant tracking systems with ease.

Built around real user data, SkillUp AI focuses on what works in 2026 - modern formatting, strong content guidance, and smart customization tools that adapt to each role and industry.

Every part of the builder is designed to save time while improving results. Users can quickly personalize layouts, adjust tone, and highlight achievements without worrying about design or formatting. The outcome is a polished, ATS-friendly resume that reflects both expertise and individuality.

ATS Checker & Job Tracker

Built-In ATS Check

  • Detects content and keyword gaps, helping bypass automated filters.
  • Compares resumes against job descriptions to highlight missing skills.
  • Calculates an alignment compliance score to guide formatting and keyword mapping.

Job Application Tracker

  • Organizes the job hunt using a Kanban method to manage applications, stages, and tasks.
  • Provides a simple, spreadsheet-like interface for easy organization.
